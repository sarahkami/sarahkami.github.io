Thesis

For my bachelor thesis I did a usability evaluation of an open source app.
I chose to conduct a heuristic evaluation in combination with simplified thinking aloud.
I asked five experts to review the app and assorted a list of possible problems.
These problems where discussed with the designer of the app
and partly transferred into github issues.
