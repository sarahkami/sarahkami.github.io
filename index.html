Evolution of a Semester Planner

With some friends I developed a semester planner for my degree course.
We first developed a paper prototype and tested it.
Based on that we created a Click-Dummy with Axure.
Finally we transferred it into a real prototype, using React and Hood.ie.
This project will be developed further
and shall become a usable support for my degree course.

Bachelor-Thesis

For my bachelor thesis I did a usability evaluation of an open source app.
I chose to conduct a heuristic evaluation in combination with simplified thinking aloud.
I asked five experts to review the app and assorted a list of possible problems.
These problems where discussed with the designer of the app
and partly transferred into github issues.
